Speed controls:

  • Tap the sun to make the earth orbit.
  • Tap the moon to make the moon orbit.
  • Tap the Earth to make it rotate.
  • Tap anywhere else to toggle shadows from the Earth and moon.

The speed controls are relative numbers representing the amount of time it takes for the object to complete its rotation--so a larger number results in a slower rotation.
